page 4	MACAO-CANTON RAILWAY.
page 4	Following the opening of the branch line of the great Conton-Handow railway now being constructed by the American China Development Company, the first practical step to give effect to the concession by H.I.M. the Emperor of China to the Portuguese Govermment whereby the line to connect the Colony of Macao by rail with the capital of South China might be an accomplished fact.
