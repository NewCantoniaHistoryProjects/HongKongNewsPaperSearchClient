page 4	MR MOORE BEGS TO RECOMMEND HIS GOGO SHAMPOO WASH TO THE PUBLIE AS UNRIVALLED BY ANY PREPARATION EVER PRODUCED FOR PROMOTING THE GROWTH OF THE HAIR.THE BASIS OF THIS COMPOUND IS MADE OF SOAP ROOT;THE NATIVES OF THE PHILIPPINE ISLANDS NEVER USE ANYTHING ELSO FOR WASHING THEIR HAIR;THEY ARE NEVER FOUND BALD,AND IT IS QUITE COMMON TO SEE THE FEMALES WITH HAIR FROM 5 TO 6 FEET LONG.
