page 2	WE have great pleasure in noting the fact that a Free Dispensary has been opened in the London Mission Society's Chapel in Taipingshau for poor Chinese. This laudable endeavour has been made by what is now known as the Hongkong Medical Mission, and every wellwisher of the Chinese will, we are sure, hail the incident with satisfaction.
