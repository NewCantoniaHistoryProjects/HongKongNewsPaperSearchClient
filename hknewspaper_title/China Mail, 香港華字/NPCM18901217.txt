page 2	If any doubt existed as to the vassalage of Corea it has been dispelled by the ceremony which took place at Seoul about a month ago. The letter of condolence from the Emperor of China to the King of Corea with reference to the death of the Queen, was made the occasion of a political demonstartion which emphasized in a marked manner China's suzerainty over the little kingdom.
