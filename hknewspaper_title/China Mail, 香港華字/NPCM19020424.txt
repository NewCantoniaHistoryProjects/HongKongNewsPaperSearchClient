page 2	The following reference to the late Dr Patrick Thurburn Manson is taken from the British Mediend Hournal of 22nd Murch:- On March 15th. a telegraphie messge brought the sad news to London thet Patrick Thurburn Manson, eldest son of Dr Patrick Manson, C.M.G.,F.R.S., had met with a fatal accident shortly after his arrival at Christmas Island.
