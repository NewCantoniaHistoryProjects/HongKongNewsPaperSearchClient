page 2	THE people who live in the pleasant places of the colony talked lightly of the Plague.  Feeling a sure immunity from any attack of the dread disease, they considered that it was not a matter which concerned them in any serious measure --- that is to say, until it affected the trade of the colony and there by interfereed with the all-imporisnt industry of dollar grinding.
