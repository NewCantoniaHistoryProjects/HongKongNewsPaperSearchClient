page 4	The members of the Hongkong Choral Society performed the original comic opera, "The Pirates of Penzarice," at the Theatre Royal, City Hall, last night. From the rush that had been made for seats, and the demand that was maintained for tickets after all the seats had been taken, a full house had been secured.
