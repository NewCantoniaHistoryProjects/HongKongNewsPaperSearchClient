page 2	WITH great regret we learn that, at a meeting of the Exeeutive Council held just before Christmas, it was decided not to adopt the recommendations contained in the Report of the Commission appointed on the 28th August last to enquire into the question of the existing difficulty of procuring and retaining reliable chair and jinricksua coolies for private chairs and jinrickshas.
